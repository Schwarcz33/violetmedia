FAQ

the details that matter

What kind of videos do you produce?

Brand introductions, service showcases, team culture videos, campaign pieces, product reveals, testimonial-style content, and social-first short-form. Every video is built from scratch — no templates, no stock footage. Original soundtrack and sound design included.

How fast is turnaround?

Typically 5–7 business days per video from brief to delivery. Retainer clients get priority scheduling, so your work never sits in a queue. Two revision rounds are included with every video.

What do I need to provide?

Your brand guidelines (logo, colours, fonts) and a brief idea of what you want each video to achieve. We handle everything else — scripting, visual production, music, sound design, and multi-format export. Your monthly strategy call is where we plan together.

What if I don’t like a video?

Every video includes 2 revision rounds. If you’re not happy after revisions, we’ll produce a replacement at no extra cost. We’d rather spend the time than deliver something you don’t love.

Can I cancel anytime?

Monthly plans can be cancelled with 14 days notice. Quarterly plans run for the committed 3-month period, then you can cancel or continue. No lock-in contracts beyond what you choose.

You use AI — is that a problem?

AI is our production instrument, not a shortcut. Every frame is directed, reviewed, and refined by a human creative director. The result is cinematic quality at a fraction of traditional costs — which is why you get 4 videos a month instead of 1 every quarter. The work speaks for itself.
